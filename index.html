Why Freelancers in Pakistan Need This

Earning in USD, EUR, or GBP means platform fees, Payoneer fees, and exchange rates all reduce your income. CalcFreelancer shows you exactly what you keep so you never undercharge. Works for Upwork, Fiverr, Freelancer, Mostaql, direct clients, or any platform.

How Platform Fees Work

Upwork charges 10%, Fiverr 20%, Freelancer.com 10%. Direct clients — no fee. You also pay 1-3% to withdraw. Always factor both when pricing.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does Upwork charge?
10% service fee plus 1-3% withdrawal fees via Payoneer or Wise.
Best withdrawal method for Pakistan?
Payoneer (direct to bank, 2-3%). Wise (lower fees, 1-2%). Bank wire ($15-25 flat).
Do Pakistani freelancers pay tax?
With PSEB registration: 0.25% ITF. Without PSEB: 0-35% standard slabs.
Can I use this for any platform?
Yes. Select a preset platform, or type any name and set your own fees.
